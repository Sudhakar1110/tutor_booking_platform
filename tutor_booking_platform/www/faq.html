Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

For Students

How do I find a tutor? ▼
You can search for tutors using the search bar on the homepage or visit the "Find Tutors" page. You can filter by subject, city, teaching mode, and more to find the perfect match for your learning needs.
Is the demo class really free? ▼
Yes! Most tutors offer a free demo class so you can experience their teaching style before committing to regular sessions. Simply request a demo class from the tutor's profile page.
How do I book a tutor? ▼
Once you've found a tutor you like, click the "Book Now" button on their profile. Fill in the booking form with your preferred schedule and requirements. The tutor will confirm your booking.
Can I change my tutor if I'm not satisfied? ▼
Absolutely! You can always search for and book a different tutor. We encourage you to use the free demo class feature to find the best match before making a long-term commitment.
How are payments handled? ▼
Payments are processed through our secure platform. You can pay via card, bank transfer, or other available methods. Payments are released to tutors after sessions are completed.

For Tutors

How do I register as a tutor? ▼
Click on "Become a Tutor" button and fill in your details including your qualifications, experience, and teaching preferences. Our team will review and verify your profile.
How do I get paid? ▼
Tutors receive payments through our secure payment system. You can track your earnings in the Tutor Dashboard and request withdrawals as per the payment schedule.
Can I set my own rates? ▼
Yes! You have complete control over your pricing. Set your hourly rate based on your experience, expertise, and market rates.
What subjects can I teach? ▼
You can teach any subject you're qualified in - from academic subjects to professional skills, programming languages, test preparation, and more.

General

Is my personal information secure? ▼
Yes, we take data security seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and protected. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.
Can I access the platform on mobile? ▼
Our website is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices including smartphones and tablets. You can access all features from any device with an internet connection.
How do I contact support? ▼
You can reach our support team through the Contact Us page, email us at support@tutorbooking.com, or call us at +91 1800-123-4567.
